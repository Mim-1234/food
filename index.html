What is the best way to store fresh produce?
To keep your produce fresh, store it in a cool, dry place, and avoid washing fruits and vegetables before storing them. Refrigerate leafy greens and berries to extend their freshness.

How do I know if meat is properly cooked?
Use a meat thermometer to check the internal temperature. Poultry should reach 165°F (75°C), and most meats like beef and pork should be cooked to at least 145°F (63°C).

What are some quick and healthy meal options?
Some quick and healthy meals include salads with lean proteins, stir-fried vegetables with tofu or chicken, and whole grain wraps with plenty of fresh vegetables and hummus.
